Role Responsibilities

Each portal role has a different responsibility. Use the correct account so records, logs, and permissions stay clean.

Clinic Staff

Handles daily clinic operations and patient-facing workflows.

  • Manage appointments and walk-in records
  • Update medical records and health profiles
  • Track inventory, treatments, and prescriptions
  • Monitor and respond to SOS alerts

Admin

Oversees campus users, reports, announcements, and operational visibility.

  • Create and manage student, faculty, and staff users
  • Review dashboards and reports
  • Broadcast announcements
  • Audit emergency and clinical activity

Super Admin

Controls high-trust settings that affect the whole system.

  • Create Admin, Clinic Staff, and ERT accounts
  • Configure maintenance and access policies
  • Manage campus map, landmarks, and SOS radius
  • Review system-wide audit logs

Daily Workflow Checklist

Use this as a quick operating rhythm before, during, and after clinic hours.

Start of Day

Check nurse availability, active appointments, low-stock medicine, pending forms, and unresolved SOS records.

During SOS

Open the SOS alert, verify location, coordinate ERT or clinic staff response, and update the case status promptly.

Inventory Movement

Record medicine additions, stock adjustments, OTC treatments, and prescription dispensing immediately.

End of Day

Review reports, confirm resolved cases, check movement logs, and sign out of shared computers.

Security and Data Handling

Use individual accounts. Avoid sharing accounts because audit logs record who created, edited, dispatched, or resolved records.

Protect patient information. Medical records and health profiles should be opened only when needed for clinic work.

Keep Super Admin actions limited. Account control, campus map settings, and maintenance mode affect multiple users and should be changed carefully.

Sign out after use. This matters especially on shared clinic computers or demonstration laptops.

Troubleshooting

If something does not load, start with access, rules, and connection checks.

Page keeps loading

Confirm the account is active and has the correct role. A permission-denied read can make dashboards appear stuck.

Notifications are not arriving

Ask the user or ERT member to log in again so the FCM token refreshes, then confirm Cloud Functions are deployed.

Map landmarks are missing

Super Admin should open Campus Map and import or add active landmarks. ERT will use fallback landmarks until managed data exists.
